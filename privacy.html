Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 · Effective April 2026 · Last updated April 1, 2026

Who We Are

Pulse provides autonomous AI operating team services to businesses. Questions about this policy: hello@pulse.ai

What Data We Collect

From website visitors: Page views and navigation data (anonymized, no personal identifiers). Form submissions when you book a Blueprint session (name, email, company, intake responses).

From customers: Access credentials to systems you grant us — minimum necessary for agreed agent functions only. Operational data processed by AI agents on your behalf (support tickets, email threads, CRM records). Call notes from Blueprint and onboarding sessions (with your consent).

How We Use Your Data

We use data to deliver and improve the AI agent services you've contracted, communicate about your service (escalations, reports, check-ins), and respond to inquiries.

We do not sell your data, use your operational data to train general AI models, or share your data with other customers.

Data Retention

Website visitors: Anonymized analytics per Plausible's standard policy.

Blueprint inquiries: Retained 12 months, or deleted on request.

Customer operational data: Retained for the duration of service plus 30 days. You may request export at any time. Deleted within 30 days of termination.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may access, correct, delete, or export your data; or object to certain processing. Email hello@pulse.ai with subject "Privacy Request." We respond within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website uses Plausible Analytics, which operates without cookies or personal identifiers. No tracking cookies, no advertising pixels.

Security

We use HTTPS/TLS for all data in transit, minimum-necessary access permissions when connecting to customer systems, and access limited to authorized Pulse personnel and AI systems. Full security overview available at hello@pulse.ai.

Changes

Material changes will be communicated to active customers by email at least 14 days before taking effect. Current version always at pulse.ai/privacy.

Contact

hello@pulse.ai

This policy is designed to meet minimum GDPR/CCPA requirements at launch. A formal legal review is recommended before scaling past 100 customers or collecting EU resident data at volume. For the current pilot phase, this document is sufficient.
